Ackvyn CRM

Git-based CRM for static sites, made portable.

Live chat, tickets, and contacts for the free Pages + git CMS world. Console and embeds from this CDN; API on a Worker you deploy — same idea as a CDN-served CMS, for customer conversations.

What is Ackvyn CRM? Deploy your Worker Why it exists

CDN GUI

Console, chat widget, and docs — always current, no SPA copy on every client site.

Your Worker

Chat, email, and visitor intake on your Cloudflare Free Worker — console CRUD stays on GitHub where it can, like Sveltia.

Git records

Encrypted contacts, tickets, and Documents in crm-data/; public reviews JSON you can ship with the site.

Drop-in embeds

One script: src from this CDN, data-crm to your Worker.

The real console

Same Messenger, Tickets, and Contacts panels as the live console — driven by a short scripted scenario. Use the timeline under the console to skip ahead. View-only; no Worker or sign-in.

This CDN

embed.js, product pages, docs, and the console GUI assets. Agents open the console from their operator site (iframe or link after sign-in) — not as a public marketing link.

Your Worker

Durable Objects, WebSockets, form intake, Email Routing — on your account. Agent ticket/contact edits prefer GitHub from the browser (usage notes).

Client sites

Marketing sites load the widget from here and talk only to your Worker.

Embed on a marketing site

<script
  src="https://crm.ackvyn.org/cdn/embed.js"
  data-site="your-site-key"
  data-crm="https://YOUR-SUBDOMAIN.workers.dev"
  data-mode="float"
  async
></script>

Never point data-crm at someone else’s Worker. Each operator deploys their own.
