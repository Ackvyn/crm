# Getting Started

1. Operator creates your **site key** in the CRM hub and allows your origin.
2. Paste `embed.js` with `data-site` and `data-crm` (see [[Embeds]]).
3. Optional: intake form → `POST /v1/{site}/intake`.
4. Optional: Google reviews → [[Reviews]].

Designed for Cloudflare Workers Free budgets — see [[Free-Tier]] /
https://crm.ackvyn.org/docs/free-tier.html

Full checklist: https://crm.ackvyn.org/docs/getting-started.html
